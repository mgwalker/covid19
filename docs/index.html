The United States has had 1,195,605 confirmed cases.
That's about 4 out of every 1,000 people who we know are or have been infected.

That is 22,152 more cases than yesterday, or a one-day increase of 1.9%. Yesterday there were 21,447 new cases, a one-day increase of 1.9%. The rate of new cases today is about the same as yesterday.

The number of confirmed cases is recently growing at about 2.2% per day. That is doubling about every 31.8 days. At the current rate, the total number of confirmed cases will be about 1,400,000 in 7 days.

The following chart shows the rate-of-change over time of different statistics. The blue line shows the rate of change in the total confirmed infections. The orange line shows the rate of change in the total number of tests administered. The black line shows the rate of change in the total number of deaths.
