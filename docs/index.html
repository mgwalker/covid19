The United States has had about 81,729 COVID-19 deaths and 1,433,696 confirmed cases. That's about 4 out of every 1,000 people who we know are or have been infected. About 5.7% of people who have tested positive have died.

There are about 1,324 new deaths every day. At this rate, about 91,000 total people will have died of COVID-19 in 7 days. This chart shows the number of deaths each day since the first case was identified. The dots are the total number of deaths for each day and the line is the average of the previous seven days.

There are about 18,657 new cases identified every day. At the current rate, the total number of confirmed cases will be about 1,600,000 in 7 days.
